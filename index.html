Weathering the Storm: Urban Consumption Responses and Resilience to Tropical Cyclones in China

Ruihua Guo, Binglin Wang

Accepted: IREUS Urban Housing Symposium 2026 (Poster) · CAERE 5th Annual Conference 2026 · CES China Annual Conference 2026

Abstract

We study how urban consumption responds to tropical cyclones in Chinese cities, combining high-frequency retail data with storm exposure. We document heterogeneous recovery patterns across sectors and characterize the role of pre-storm economic conditions in shaping resilience.

How Subsidized Shared Transport Shapes Urban Mobility Patterns: Evidence from Cellphone Mobility Data in Los Angeles

Ruihua Guo, Jingwei Zhang, Wengdong Zhang

Working paper

Abstract

Using anonymized cellphone mobility data for Los Angeles, we estimate how subsidies to shared transport modes reshape trip patterns, mode substitution, and accessibility. We quantify distributional effects across neighborhoods and identify conditions under which subsidies crowd in versus crowd out existing transit use.
